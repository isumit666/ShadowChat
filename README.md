## 🌌 Vision & Philosophy
**ShadowChat** is not just another messaging platform; it is a meticulously crafted **VVIP Experience**. Designed and architected by **Sumit**, this application breaks the boundaries of standard UI/UX by blending **OLED dark aesthetics, glassmorphism, and cinematic interactions**. It is built for users who demand absolute privacy wrapped in an ultra-premium interface.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🎭 Next-Gen Interface (VisionOS Style)
* **OLED Optimized:** Pure black backgrounds (`#000000`) with vibrant neon accents to save battery and reduce eye strain.
* **Glassmorphism:** Multi-layered blurred backgrounds (`backdrop-filter`) for a premium frosted glass effect.
* **Squircle Geometry:** Apple-style rounded squares for all buttons and modules, ensuring visual harmony.

### 🎥 Cinematic Screen Sharing (`stream.html`)
* **Direct Presentation:** Share your screen directly with an integrated audio call.
* **PiP Mode:** Seamless Picture-in-Picture mode for your front camera while presenting.
* **Vignette Effect:** Cinematic shadows around the edges to keep the focus centered on the stream.

### 📁 1GB Ultra Media Share (`media.html`)
* **Direct Gallery Access:** One-tap hidden triggers to natively open the phone's gallery and file manager.
* **Real-time Chunk Simulation:** Satisfying progress bars with neon shimmer effects for large file uploads.

### 🔒 Uncompromising Security
* Military-grade **E2E Encrypted** badges.
* **HD Voice** indicators with active heartbeat pulse animations.
